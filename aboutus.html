History of FURNID ?

Furnid is one of the biggest furniture company in Indonesia. Estabilished at 2005, Furnid is founded by Mr. Thomas Lee, a korean-indonesian who lived in Bandung. Pastly, Furnid is only a odd jobs around household furniture. Mr. Thomas says that before Furnid developed as this big, he start it by knocking people's door house by house. He get a lot of insluts while do it, but for him, shame can help him to grew his children. Slowly, his bussines started to went well and he tried to build Furnid slowly until Furnid be as this big.

What is Furnid?

Furnid is the company that work in furniture. We sell so many household things, such as:table, chair, drawer, wardrobes, multipurpose cabinets, decorative lights, and others. Also since 2012, Furnic started to build a little cafetaria in every Furnid stand. We hope by doing this, our costumers will feel freely to shopping and can reach our cafe anytime when tired and hungry. Our motto is Costumer's pleasant is number 1 and our vision is will be the best Furniture company in Indonesia at 2030.
